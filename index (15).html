About the System

Built for Eket, Powered by Community

Bridging the gap between citizens and law enforcement in Ikot Udota, Eket Local Government Area, Akwa Ibom State.

The Ikot Udota Division of the Nigeria Police Force serves the Eket Local Government Area of Akwa Ibom State, which includes Ikot Udota — home to Heritage Polytechnic and a growing community.

This system provides a fast, secure, and accessible digital channel for citizens to report incidents, receive community safety alerts, and communicate with the police — reducing response time and increasing public confidence in law enforcement.

The platform is powered by Google Firebase — meaning every report submitted goes live instantly to the police admin dashboard from any device, anywhere in Eket LGA.

Project Information

About This Project

ND2 Project — Computer Science Department, Heritage Polytechnic, Ikot Udota, Eket, Akwa Ibom State.

This website is the implementation component of the project titled "Design and Implementation of a Crime Reporting and Alert Management System", submitted for the award of a National Diploma (ND) at Heritage Polytechnic, Ikot Udota, Eket, Akwa Ibom State.

The system is powered by Google Firebase — a real-time cloud database that allows crime reports submitted by citizens to appear instantly on the police admin dashboard, from any device, anywhere in the world.
